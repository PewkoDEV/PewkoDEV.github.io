Who we are

Pewko Studios takes its name from Pewko, my online alias. Behind that name, there is no team and no promise — just me.

I make games because I care about worlds, systems, and stories that take their time. Not to chase trends, not to make noise, but to build something coherent and sincere.

Pewko Studios was born from that simple desire: to make games with care, at my own pace, without unnecessary compromises. If others ever join the journey, it will be for the same reasons. For now, everything begins here.

Make things that matter

Every system, every story, every choice — we want it to feel alive and meaningful. Nothing thrown together.

Respect players

We trust you to explore, experiment, and figure things out. No hand-holding. No fluff. Just fun.

Slow and steady

We work carefully. Fast releases are tempting, but we’d rather do it right.

Projects

For now, Pewko Studios doesn’t show anything. There’s no game ready to explore, just ideas that are slowly taking shape and mechanics I’m testing. I’m still thinking about what the first game will be, but I already know it will be narrative, immersive, and crafted with care.

I don’t want false hype or empty promises. Everything will come in time.
